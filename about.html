About Just Show Up

We created Just Show Up because planning dates can kill the spark. One person always ends up doing all the work, and the other waits... and waits. We wanted something different: effortless, spontaneous moments that feel thoughtful without the stress.

Whether you're surprising your partner with "I already picked the spot — just meet me", or you're both free and want something fun now, we scan local events, pop-ups, and vibes in your area and give you perfect, low-pressure options. No RSVPs, no overthinking — just show up and enjoy.

Built in Arlington, Texas — but made for anywhere. Let's bring back real connection, one unplanned night at a time.
