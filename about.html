About Us

We started Just Show Up because planning dates shouldn't feel like a second job.

One person always ends up doing all the thinking, researching, booking — and the spark gets lost in the stress. We wanted something better: effortless, spontaneous, thoughtful moments that just happen.

Tell us your vibe, your zip code, and what you're feeling — we scan what's happening nearby right now and give you perfect low-pressure options. No RSVPs, no overthinking, no planning. Just show up and enjoy.

Built in Arlington, Texas — but made for anywhere. Let's bring back real connection, one unplanned night at a time.
